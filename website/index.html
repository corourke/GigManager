Features

Everything your production needs,
in one place

From the first date-hold to the final paycheck, GigWrangler handles the full lifecycle of every event.

🎪

Full Gig Lifecycle

Track every gig from Date-Hold through Advance, Confirmed, Show Day, and Paid — with automatic status history logging at every transition.

🏢

Multi-Org Collaboration

Multiple production companies, sound crews, lighting vendors, and venues can collaborate on the same gig while keeping their own data completely private.

🔊

Equipment & Kit Management

Track owned assets, build loadout kits, assign gear to gigs, and automatically detect scheduling conflicts before they become problems.

👥

Personnel Scheduling

Define staff roles, assign crew to gigs, send confirmation notifications, and detect double-bookings across your entire calendar.

📅

Google Calendar Sync

OAuth-based two-way sync keeps your gig schedule reflected in Google Calendar automatically — no manual entry required.

📱

Mobile & Offline-First

A full PWA experience with biometric lock, QR code scanning for inventory, push notifications, and offline access — built for warehouse and show-day use.

💰

Financial Tracking

Attach budgets, track expenses, and manage purchases per gig. Rental and insurance tracking included so nothing falls through the cracks.

📂

CSV Import / Export

Migrate from spreadsheets in minutes. Bulk-import gigs, assets, personnel, and kits via CSV with validation and error reporting built in.

⚡

Real-Time Updates

WebSocket-powered live data means every team member sees changes the moment they happen — no refreshing, no stale data on show day.

How it works

From enquiry to invoice in four steps

GigWrangler is designed around the way production people already work — just without the chaos.

1

Create your gig

Add a gig with the client, venue, date, and any participating organisations. It starts as a Date-Hold automatically.

2

Build your loadout

Assign equipment kits and staff roles. Conflict detection flags any double-bookings before they become problems.

3

Advance & confirm

Progress the gig through Advance and Confirmed stages. Send crew confirmations and sync to Google Calendar.

4

Show day & wrap

Use the mobile scanner for inventory check-out/in. Mark the gig Complete and Paid with a full audit trail retained.
